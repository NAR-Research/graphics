Where movers come from, and where they go next?

Charleston, SC entered 2026 as one of the top housing hot spots identified by NAR Research, where new buyer opportunities are emerging this year. With strong income growth (6% higher than the previous year) and solid job gains (3.2% job growth rate), the Southeastern market is attracting young adults and families to the area.

Charleston–North Charleston, S.C.
Characteristics of movers

■ 56.5% of movers moved within South Caroline while 36.4% relocated from a different state.

■ 7.1% of movers moved within the same county in the Charleston metropolitan area.


Most popular areas

■ Charlotte-Concord-Gastonia, NC-SC was the leading origin metro for inbound movers, representing 7.5% of total movers to Charleston.

■ Greenville-Anderson, SC was the most popular destination for Charleston households, with 19.5% of movers relocating there.
